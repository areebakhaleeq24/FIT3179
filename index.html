Foodborne Disease Outbreaks

Statistics of USA from 1985 to 2015.

Likely sources of foodborne diseases

The donut chart represents the places where people are most likely to get these diseases. Restaurants happen to be the most common source/place where people get infected as it constitutes more than 50% of the possible places. It is a pity that the very place which needs to mantain the strictest hygiene protocols, ends up affecting a large population!

Occurence of foodborne diseases over around 30 years

The line chart shows the trend of foodborne diseases from 1998 to 2015. It is unfortunate that the change in trend is quite drastic over the years, which makes it almost impossible to predict a future trend.

Months with a greater probability of getting infected by foodborne diseases

The bat chart aims to highlight the months which record the highest number of diseases. Such information can be helpful in understanding trends in order to avoid future outbreaks. An example can be avoiding fish in bredding seasons incase it is known to contribute to higher cases
