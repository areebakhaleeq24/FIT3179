Foodborne Disease Outbreaks in USA

A closer look at the cases with respect to each state.

Restaurants correspond to the greatest number of confirmed cases. Unfortunately, the very place which should mantain the highest hygiene protocols, ends up infecting a major chunk of the population! Furthermore, the trend of foodborne disease outbreaks is drastic & unpredictable, making it difficult to predict future outbreaks.

The spread of illnesses over different months and over a span of over around 30 years provides helpful insights in the process of data analysis, which is helpful in understanding trends. Months can help identify the root cause of the outbreak in special cases such as a disease associated with fish in its breeding months could give an indication of the fact that their consumption needs to be halted for a specific period of time.
